KAYMO THITIMA

Artist Bio

About Me

William Kamore Githire a.k.a Kaymo Thitima, is born and bred in a humble Christian back ground.

He is an Multi-Award Winning Artist, with many titles to His Name, i.e (Professor Kamore)

He is working on His Current Album, among them with His New Hit Single KANASHIKA, this is after Glowing in the the Music scene with his Hit songs, THITIMA ANTHEM released on August 2016 and GATHIGIRIRI song, released on May 2017 Respectively.

He is a full time Producer and a Performing Artist and Serves in His Local Church (Karura Community Chapel) in Praise and Worship.

Production

He discovered his interest in music at a very young age.

He is highly trained and skilled in diverse musical instruments, sound engineering and music production.

Being a Co-Fonder of Kamata Music School Kenya in 2006, gained him more than ten years of extensive experience in teaching and managerial skills of business.

He also took part in founding the; THE SAMAWATI BAND which was at the pick of its success in 2012-2016. Being the main Drummer, a Vocalist, Sound Engineer and Music producer of The Samawati band, his contribution is absolutely undisputable

Hence, He set out to Build and set up a Production Studio, Elea Entertainment that has set out to bring Quality Music, Ambience into film, Sound bed into TVC, & Voicing of Documentaries.

Achievments

His social skills and ability to work with people and maintain good long lasting relationships is way past the bar. Having worked with different artists and producers it has gained him Good Equity and Stability in the music industry.

The Multi Award winning influencer has had great opportunities to meet and work in partnership with the Office Of the President of Kenya, and that of the Deputy President as well, through an Individual Initiative called G-VOTIE whereby He as the Ambassador advocated for the youth to become registered voters so as to be able to have a voice in their own country.

Ambassador

Kaymo Thitima is a Certified Peace Ambassador by the Kenya Peace-Co Foundation, through his effort to travel to different regions preaching peace and harmony.

Kaymo Thitima is now considered a serious contributor in the shaping of African Modern Musical Sound, an Icon and a role model to many of the younger generation, with a spark of creative metaphors and lines in his music,

He has inspired and simplified the message of hope and Love to the Human Society and brought Joy where in some instances would have been considered tragic ends.
